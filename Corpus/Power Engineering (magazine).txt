Power EngineeringManaging EditorRussell RayCategoriesScienceFrequencyMonthlyPublisherPennWell CorporationYear founded1896CountryUnited StatesBased inTulsa, OklahomaLanguageEnglishWebsitewww.power-eng.comISSN0032-5961 Power Engineering is a monthly magazine dedicated to professionals in the field of power engineering and power generation.[1] Articles are focused on new developments in power plant design, construction and operation in North America. Power Engineering is published by PennWell Corporation, the largest U.S. publisher of electric power industry books, directories, maps and conferences.
